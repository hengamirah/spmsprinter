Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about orders and delivery.

How do I claim my study deck? ▼
Visit our claim page, enter your Shopee Order ID, and follow the prompts to receive your deck. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes.
Why can't I claim my order yet? (Release Delay) ▼
To prevent fraud, we require a 30-minute verification period after your order is placed. You'll see a countdown timer on the claim page showing exactly when your order will be ready. This is a security measure we use to protect both our customers and content.
I entered my Order ID but it says "Not Found" ▼
The Order ID must match exactly what's shown in your Shopee purchase history. Make sure you:
  • Copied the exact Order ID (it's case-insensitive)
  • Didn't include extra spaces
  • Ordered from the correct seller
If you're still stuck, contact us.
Where is my email? (Gmail tips) ▼
The deck usually arrives within 5 minutes. If you haven't received it:
  • Check spam/junk folder — some email providers filter PDFs
  • Make sure you used the right email — we can't resend to a different address
  • Check your email storage — if your inbox is full, emails may bounce
  • Avoid school/student emails — they often block attachments (use Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook)
If the email still doesn't arrive after 30 minutes, let us know.
Can I get the deck sent to a different email? ▼
Unfortunately, no. For security reasons, each Order ID can only deliver once. To avoid this, make sure you enter the correct email address on the first try. If you made a mistake, please contact us via Shopee chat.
What format is the study deck in? How do I view it? ▼
The study decks are delivered as HTML files that you can open directly in your web browser. No special software needed!

How to use:
  • Windows/Mac: Double-click the file → opens in your default browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox, etc.)
  • Android: Download the file → open with any web browser app
  • iPhone: We recommend downloading Microsoft Edge from the App Store (works best with HTML files) or use Safari

✅ Works offline! Once downloaded, you can view it anytime without internet access — perfect for studying on the go.
The email says it's a bundle. What does that mean? ▼
A bundle means your order includes multiple study decks (e.g., "Add Maths + Physics"). You'll receive all of them in the same email as separate PDF attachments.
How long are the study decks valid? Can I access them later? ▼
Once you download the PDF, it's yours to keep forever. You can access it anytime on any device. We recommend saving it in a safe location or cloud storage (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox) so you don't lose it.
Can I share the study deck with friends? ▼
Our study decks are for personal use only. Each person should purchase their own copy to support our work and ensure we can keep creating quality materials. Sharing copies violates our terms of service.
Still have questions? ▼
We're here to help! Contact us or reach out to us via Shopee chat and we'll get back to you quickly.
